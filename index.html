Work

My research focuses on understanding phonons and electron-phonon interactions in 2D materials.

Phonons in moiré systems
Electron-phonon in moiré
AuAg nanoparticles
In moiré systems, first principles computations of phonons is prohibitively expensive due to the large number of atoms in the unit cell. A suitable workaround to first principles calculations have been found via the use of classical force fields, fitted against DFT calculations give accurate phononic band structures. But the currently available codes utilizing these force fields are not equipped to handle large systems. We have written a package PARPHOM for computing the phonon bands in systems with tens of thousands of atoms.
